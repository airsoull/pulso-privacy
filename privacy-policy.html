Overview

Pulso is a mood tracking app designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything you record stays exclusively on your device.

Data Collection

Pulso collects no data whatsoever. The app has no backend, no servers, and makes no network requests. Your mood entries and notes are stored locally on your device using SQLite and are never transmitted anywhere.

Biometric Authentication

Pulso offers an optional Face ID / Touch ID lock. Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (iOS or Android). Pulso never accesses, stores, or processes your biometric data in any form.

Third-Party Services

Pulso does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app contains a link to Ko-fi (a voluntary support page) that opens in your device's browser when tapped. No data is shared with Ko-fi unless you choose to visit that page.

Children's Privacy

Since Pulso does not collect any data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
